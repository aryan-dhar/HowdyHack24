Welcome to our Mental Health Peer Support Forum, a platform dedicated to providing a safe and supportive space for individuals to discuss mental health topics. Our website is built with the aim of promoting openness and understanding through anonymous peer-to-peer interactions. Here, users can create posts, share their stories, and provide support to others through comments.

The website offers:

  • Anonymized peer support through forum-style discussions.
  • Dedicated pages with resources for mental health issues, including depression and suicide hotlines.
  • A safe space to share personal experiences without judgment.
We used several technologies to bring this project to life, including:
  • HTML & CSS for structuring and styling the webpage.
  • JavaScript for dynamic content and interactions.
  • Node.js for backend development and managing server-side operations.
  • Express.js to handle routing and APIs.
  • MongoDB for storing and retrieving posts and comments in a database.
